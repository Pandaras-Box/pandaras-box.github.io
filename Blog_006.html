PANDARA'S BOX

Pandara’s on tour (Red Spear in-hand!) to Develop: Brighton & discussion on intuitive inputs and art direction…

Last week the idea of “Pandara’s Box on tour” was mentioned, coined by David and his meddlesome edits (E.N: Careful now.). Of course, what this actually meant was that I, along with Red Spear’s Matthew Lagan(@Ghostlyy), travelled over to the wonderfully sunny Brighton for @developconf. While trying to regurgitate verbatim our muddled, half-forgotten and mutated versions of the full contents of each talk we attended is probably not going to benefit anyone, the roster provided some very interesting key talking points to touch upon. Back here in the grey dimness of Belfast (home sweet home), debates are building over how to take our structurally sound (heh (E.N: down with this sort of thing.)) mechanics and sprinkle some much needed creative spark into how players interact with them. Meanwhile, with the jet-lag clear and enthusiasm high, Ciaran (@_charper) has been making great strides in locking down the art direction of the project.

TL;DR: Calm week, Pandaras on tour, project accumulation, Overwatch! Overwatch!
