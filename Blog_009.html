PANDARA'S BOX

Rise of the Fall of the Dawn of Project Leitmotif (i.e. #Tranzfuser is ending! Onwards we go… )

It’s genuinely baffling to me that this is the 9th piece of rambling text that I’ve produced, with help from @Thierymodela of course, for the bemused reading of all 5 of you (Hi mom!) since we at @Pandaras_Box embarked on #Tranzfuser. Somehow I think I’d failed to mention that, in fact, I had wanted way back during the development of #Slimebow to start putting a dev blog of sorts together, yet it took until we finished our degrees before it surfaced! (E.N: Jury’s still out on if it was worth the wait.)

It’s certainly been an interesting 9 and a half weeks, and it all began with an idea. The idea was to bring together a group of acceptably useful people to see if they could become something more.

I take a great deal of delight in how fortunate it was that the folks of @UKGamesTalent decided that 2016 should be the year they launch their pilot for a graduate scheme. While the risk was always there that we would end up as (occasionally bearded) guinea pigs, the work their team has put into keeping everything running smooth has been great and their being available wonderfully quickly when any queries have arisen has been of fantastic help. The scheme itself has been a great learning experience, and in some aspects a rude awakening for us as a team, much of this being linked to the nature of the process. What do I mean by that, I desperately hope you’re thinking? Well, in essence, Tranzfuser has been a concept and prototyping period of development on steroids! With the condensed time-frame, we’ve witnessed more milestones, redesigns and breakthroughs (E.N: and breakdowns, right Conall?) of a technical, artistic and design-based nature than I would presume most early-stage projects would hope to see during the first few weeks of their team existing, let-alone the first few weeks of a project.

With only a few days left, and plenty of stuff we want to get polished, I’m actually going to wrap up SUPER early this week, though you can look forward to a bumper edition next week, with Vlog 2 included!.
