R. H. Itanic!

In a desolate future where the RHI scandal has eliminated the human race, a single boat attempts to escape the perils of global warming. Plot twist - the captain is Marty McGuiness. Can he reach Stormont and collapse the government? Click/tap on the water to send out shockwaves that will knock away oncoming Icebergs from the heroic vessel SS RHItanic!!
