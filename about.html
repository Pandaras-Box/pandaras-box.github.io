Who are Pandara's Box?

The core trifecta of game developers that makes the core of the team originally formed late 2013 for their placement years (2x MEng GameDev/1x BEng CompSci graduates ]– Queen’s University Belfast), and subsequently, worked on a variety of projects, including Slimebow (iOS/Android) and a funded project from Northern Ireland's Arts Council. Concurrently, the team were Student finalists of NISPConnect’s INVENT 2014. After finishing their education, the core team reformed in mid-2016 upon successful entrance into Tranzfuser, expanding to five members, including another BEng CompSci graduate (QUB) and a BA Fine Art graduate (Ulster University) (who had done so with a sell-out, award-winning graduation art show). Their current major project, started as part of Tranzfuser is called Project Leitmotif, though you probably knew that already if you've made it as far as the "About" section of their site! ;)
